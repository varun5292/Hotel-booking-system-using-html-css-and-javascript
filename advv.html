Amenities


Swimming Pool

Our resort's swimming pool is a refreshing oasis for guests to unwind and enjoy leisurely swims. With its spacious design, comfortable sun loungers, and poolside bar, it offers a perfect blend of relaxation and recreation, creating an enjoyable and memorable experience for all visitors.

Spa

Relax and rejuvenate at our luxurious spa with a wide range of treatments.Our resort's spa is a sanctuary of relaxation and rejuvenation. Guests can indulge in a wide range of luxurious treatments, including massages, facials, and body therapies, provided by skilled therapists. It offers a serene ambiance and personalized services to enhance overall well-being and tranquility.

Fitness Center

Stay fit in our fully-equipped fitness center with state-of-the-art equipment.Our resort's gym is a fully-equipped fitness center designed to cater to all fitness levels. With state-of-the-art equipment and professional trainers, guests can engage in various workouts, stay active, and maintain their health and well-being during their stay at the resort.

Party Hall

Our resort's party hall is the perfect venue for hosting unforgettable events. With its spacious layout, elegant decor, and state-of-the-art facilities, it provides an ideal setting for weddings, parties, and other special occasions, ensuring a memorable and enjoyable experience for all attendees.

Gaming Room

Our resort's gaming room is a haven for entertainment enthusiasts. Featuring a variety of games and consoles, it offers a fun-filled space for guests to indulge in friendly competitions and enjoy immersive gaming experiences, making it a perfect recreational spot for all ages.

Movie Theater

Our resort's movie theater is a cinematic retreat where guests can immerse themselves in the world of film. Equipped with comfortable seating and high-quality audiovisual systems, it offers a captivating movie-watching experience, perfect for relaxing and enjoying the latest blockbusters or timeless classics.
